Nahodha Rising

Awakening the sleeping giant

Nahodha Rising is a community-based organization focused on mentoring, empowering, and transforming boys and girls through education, life skills, mentorship, and character development.

Founded in 2023, we support children aged 3 to 18 through weekly, monthly, and holiday programs that restore direction, discipline, and purpose.

Why Nahodha Rising Exists

We were started to address neglected child development, especially among boys facing substance abuse, poor role models, and limited mentorship support systems.

  • Drug abuse and negative peer influence
  • School dropout and broken family support
  • Identity crisis and low self-esteem
  • Rising indiscipline, violence, and hopelessness
  • Lack of structured mentorship and accountability

Without guidance, many children fall into destructive habits, poor decision-making, and unfulfilled potential.

Our Story

Nahodha Rising was started by passionate community leaders and mentors burdened by the growing challenges affecting boys and vulnerable children.

We recognized that many boys lacked direction and were vulnerable to harmful influences because positive male guidance was missing.

Mentorship provides guidance, accountability, emotional support, and direction that children need to thrive.

Our goal is to raise responsible, disciplined, God-fearing, respectful, and confident leaders in the community.
