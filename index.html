Raising Boys Into Responsible Men

Nahodha Rising is a Christian mentorship movement in Kenya guiding boys and young men into adulthood through faith, discipline, brotherhood, and leadership.

We walk with them early — before the streets, confusion, and pressure define their future.

Why Nahodha Rising Exists

Many boys are growing up without the structure, mentorship, and guidance they need to become stable, responsible men.

  • Lack of consistent male mentorship
  • Exposure to harmful environments too early
  • School dropout and unemployment pressure
  • Identity confusion and lack of direction
  • Emotional isolation and silence around struggle

Without guidance, many young men drift into patterns that affect families, communities, and their own future potential.

Our Story

Nahodha Rising began with a simple conviction — boys don’t become strong men by accident.

For years, much of the public attention and support has centered on girls, while many boys have been left without real support systems, safe guidance, or consistent mentorship.

Without that support, some drift into alcohol, hopelessness, violence, and other destructive paths.

We are building a mentorship journey that shapes character, strengthens faith, and develops leadership from a young age.
