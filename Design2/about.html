Why Nahodha Rising Exists

Nahodha Rising exists to restore direction, discipline, and hope to young people in Kenya, with our work mostly focused on boys, through Christian mentorship and community support.

While many support efforts have rightly focused on girls, our work centers mostly on supporting boys while also serving other community groups who need structure, safe spaces, and consistent guidance.

Our Story

We saw a growing gap: too many young people—particularly boys—were navigating pressure, loss of identity, and isolation without real support systems around them.

Without trusted mentors and stable community, many boys and other young people end up learning from the wrong voices and drifting into drinking, hopelessness, violence, and other destructive habits.

Nahodha Rising was formed to respond with faith, discipline, and practical mentorship that helps boys and other young people become responsible adults.

Vision, Mission, and Values

Vision

To raise a generation of young people—mostly boys—who are spiritually grounded, disciplined, and ready to lead with integrity.

Mission

To mentor boys and other young people through faith, education, life skills, and community-centered support.

Core Values

Faith, discipline, responsibility, humility, and service.

Our Long-Term Goal

We want young people, particularly boys, to grow up with the support, structure, and confidence they need to build healthy families and strong communities.

Faith

Grounding identity in Christ.

Growth

Building character and discipline.

Impact

Raising men who serve others well.
