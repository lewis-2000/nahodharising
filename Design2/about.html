Why Nahodha Rising Exists

Nahodha Rising is a community-based organization focused on mentoring, empowering, and transforming boys and girls through education, life skills, mentorship, and character development.

We were started in 2023 to address increasing challenges facing children, especially boys, including school dropout, poor role models, substance abuse, and lack of mentorship.

Our Story

Nahodha Rising was founded by passionate community leaders and mentors burdened by the neglect of child development and the growing crisis among vulnerable boys.

We observed rising indiscipline, hopelessness, peer pressure, identity crisis, and emotional struggles among children in our communities.

The realization was clear: many boys lacked direction and were vulnerable to harmful influences without positive guidance and accountability.

Vision, Mission, and Values

Vision

To raise a generation of young people—mostly boys—who are spiritually grounded, disciplined, and ready to lead with integrity.

Mission

To mentor boys and other young people through faith, education, life skills, and community-centered support.

Core Values

Integrity, equality, inclusion, discipline, responsibility, empowerment, and respect.

Guiding Scripture

Proverbs 22:6 - "Train up a child in the way he should go..."

1 Timothy 4:12 - "Set an example for the believers..."

Success for us means seeing children excel academically, build strong character, avoid harmful behaviors, and become leaders in their communities.

Our Long-Term Goal

We want young people, particularly boys, to grow up with the support, structure, and confidence they need to build healthy families and strong communities.

Faith

Grounding identity in Christ.

Growth

Building character and discipline.

Impact

Raising men who serve others well.
