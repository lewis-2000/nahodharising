Our Programs

Nahodha Rising mentors and empowers children through structured programs focused on education, life skills, character development, and leadership.

Current Activities

Programs are run weekly, monthly, and during school holidays for children aged 3 to 18 years.

Mentorship Sessions

Guidance on discipline, leadership, goal setting, and personal development.

Educational Support

Academic encouragement, school engagement, and support for improved attendance and performance.

Guidance and Counseling

Emotional support and practical help to navigate peer pressure, identity struggles, and difficult environments.

Life Skills Training

Training in responsibility, communication, decision making, and character.

Drug Abuse Awareness

Prevention campaigns that educate children, parents, and communities on substance abuse risks.

Sports and Team Building

Community activities that build discipline, confidence, teamwork, and healthy relationships.

Future Activities

  • Leadership camps
  • Vocational training opportunities
  • Expanded community outreach
  • Scholarship support initiatives
  • Sports mentorship programs

Camps, workshops, and seminars are organized periodically to deepen impact across schools, churches, and communities.

Program Impact

Parents and mentors report visible growth in discipline, confidence, leadership, and responsibility among participating children.

Behavior Change

Improved discipline, focus, and self-awareness.

Academic Growth

Better school attendance and classroom performance.

Healthier Relationships

Stronger relationships with parents, teachers, and peers.
