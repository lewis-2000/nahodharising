Nahodha Rising

Awakening the sleeping giant

Nahodha Rising is a community-based movement focused on mentoring, empowering, and transforming boys and girls through education, life skills, mentorship, and character development.

Why Nahodha Rising Exists

We respond to neglected child development, especially for boys facing substance abuse, weak support systems, and poor role models.

Core Pressure Points

Drug abuse, peer pressure, school dropout, identity crisis, and emotional struggles.

What We Do

Mentorship sessions, educational support, guidance and counseling, life skills, and drug abuse awareness.

Founding Story

Started by committed community leaders and mentors burdened by the crisis affecting vulnerable children.

Guiding Scripture

Proverbs 22:6 and 1 Timothy 4:12 shape our mentorship and leadership model.

Future Vision

A leading child empowerment movement reaching schools, churches, informal settlements, and underserved rural communities.

Our Goal in One Line

Raise a generation of responsible, disciplined, respectful, confident, and productive young leaders.
