Why Nahodha Rising Exists

Many boys and other youth are growing up without stable support, guidance, or strong role models. We build that missing structure.

Core Pressure Points

Identity confusion, dropout pressure, and emotional isolation.

What We Do

Mentorship circles, faith formation, leadership training, life skills, and outreach.

Our Story in One Line

Strong adults do not emerge by accident, so we intervene early with intentional mentorship and community accountability.
