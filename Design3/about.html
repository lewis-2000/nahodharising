Why We Exist

About Nahodha Rising

A community-based movement founded in 2023 to mentor and empower children, especially boys, through education, life skills, mentorship, and character formation.

Observation

Too many boys were navigating identity pressure, dropout risk, and harmful environments without mentors.

Response

We built mentorship circles shaped by faith, discipline, responsibility, and practical life skills.

Growth

Families, churches, schools, and volunteers joined to sustain child development and community impact.

Vision

Raise grounded young people, mostly boys, ready to lead with integrity and positive influence in society.

Mission

Mentor through faith, education, life skills, and community-centered support.

Core Values

Integrity | Equality | Inclusion | Discipline | Responsibility | Empowerment | Respect

Guiding Scripture

Proverbs 22:6 and 1 Timothy 4:12 anchor our child mentorship approach.

What Success Looks Like

Children excelling academically, building strong character, and becoming responsible leaders in their communities.
