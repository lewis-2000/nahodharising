Stand With Young People — mostly boys

Nahodha Rising is building a support system for boys and other young people who need guidance and a faith-centered path forward.

You can help by volunteering, mentoring, partnering, praying, or supporting programs that keep young men grounded and accountable.

Ways to Help

Volunteer

Support events, mentorship sessions, outreach visits, and community activities.

Mentor

Walk with boys and other young people consistently and help them grow in discipline, faith, and responsibility.

Partner

Churches, schools, and organizations can collaborate with us to reach more boys and other youth.

Give

Donations help fund workshops, transport, learning materials, and outreach programs.

Pray

Spiritual support matters. Pray for boys and other young people, mentors, families, and the communities we serve.

Refer

Point parents, church leaders, and young people—especially boys—toward the program so they can join the journey.

Who We Want to Reach

Boys and other young people, parents, church leaders, mentors, volunteers, and sponsors who believe young people deserve intentional support.

We are especially focused on boys and other young people who feel unseen, unsupported, or pulled toward alcohol, peer pressure, and confusion.

Your involvement helps us replace isolation with structure and hope.

Get in touch

If you want to volunteer, partner, or learn more, reach out and we’ll help you find the best way to serve.
