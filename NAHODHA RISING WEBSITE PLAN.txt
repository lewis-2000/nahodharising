NAHODHA RISING WEBSITE PLAN
===========================

PROJECT DESCRIPTION
-------------------
Nahodha Rising is a Christian mentorship and leadership organization focused on guiding boys and young men in Kenya toward responsible adulthood through faith, mentorship, discipline, brotherhood, education, and community support.

The organization exists to address the growing neglect of boys in society, helping them avoid destructive paths such as alcoholism, hopelessness, violence, poor mentorship, and lack of direction. The goal is to help boys become productive, spiritually grounded, responsible men who positively impact their families, churches, and communities.

The website should feel:
- Modern
- Hopeful
- Masculine but welcoming
- African and authentic
- Community-driven
- Spiritually grounded
- Professional and trustworthy

The website should NOT feel:
- Politically divisive
- Anti-girl
- Outdated
- Overly corporate
- Depressing
- Like a generic church website


PRIMARY WEBSITE GOALS
---------------------
- Clearly explain the mission of Nahodha Rising
- Build trust and credibility
- Showcase mentorship programs and impact
- Allow frequent posting of updates and blog posts
- Encourage volunteering and partnerships
- Make communication easy
- Build a strong modern identity for the organization


TARGET AUDIENCE
